Our Mission

Approximately 2.6 billion lack opportunity to access technology and the opportunity to learn how to use it.

Our mission? To shrink that number — by making digital education accessible, empowering communities with code and AI, and unlocking potential across every corner of the world.

We’re a nonprofit dedicated to creating opportunity and impact by equipping future innovators everywhere with essential digital skills.
